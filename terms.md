## The app

Tilth is a garden tracking tool. It helps you log beds, plants, soil
conditions, and weather, and uses an AI model to help diagnose plant
problems. Your garden data is stored locally on your device — we do not
operate a database of your personal information.

## AI features

Diagnostic queries use Anthropic's Claude API. When you run a
diagnosis, relevant garden context (plant, bed, soil, weather, your
concern) is sent to the API. Tilth does not send your name, email, or
any personally identifying information with these requests. See our
[Privacy Policy](privacy) for the full breakdown of what is sent and
when.

AI responses are informational. They are not a substitute for advice
from a certified horticulturalist or agronomist. Results may vary based
on the data you've logged.
