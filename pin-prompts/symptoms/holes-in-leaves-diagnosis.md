## The 6 diagnosis panels
1. Slugs → Irregular holes anywhere on the plant
2. Flea beetles → Tiny round shotgun-pattern holes, often on eggplant, brassicas, or radish
3. Cabbage worms and loopers → Large ragged holes in brassicas
4. Deer or rabbits → Clean diagonal cuts on stems
5. Earwigs → Irregular holes on leaves plus damage to flowers and ripening fruit
6. Other caterpillars → Frass on leaves but no immediately visible bug
