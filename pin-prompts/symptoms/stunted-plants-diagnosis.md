7 causes, one fast first check

## Diagnostic callout
First check: All plants stunted → it's the bed, the soil, the spot. One plant stunted, others fine → it's individual. Pull it gently. White and bushy roots → healthy roots, look elsewhere (pest, environmental)

## The 7 diagnosis panels
1. Insufficient sun → Leggy growth reaching toward light, small thin leaves, no flowering or fruiting
2. Nutrient deficiency → Plant is pale and small overall
3. Cool soil → Heat-loving plants refuse to grow until soil warms
4. Root-bound → Container-grown plant whose roots have circled the pot and choked themselves
5. Soil compaction → Plant in heavy or compacted soil; roots can't penetrate
6. Root pests → Plant looks stalled with no above-ground explanation
7. Transplant shock → Recently moved or repotted; growth stalled for 2–3 weeks before resuming
