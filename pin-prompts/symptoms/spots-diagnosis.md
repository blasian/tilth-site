## The 7 diagnosis panels
1. Powdery mildew → White, flour-dusted patches on leaf surfaces
2. Septoria leaf spot → Small brown circles with yellow halos and a tan center
3. Early blight → Brown spots with concentric bullseye rings, often surrounded by yellowing
4. Late blight → Large, irregular dark patches on leaves, often dark brown to black with a fuzzy or oily
5. Bacterial spot → Small dark spots that look greasy or water-soaked, often with yellow halos
6. Sunscald → Whitish, papery patches on fruits exposed to direct sun, especially after pruning that removed the leaves
7. Water spots → Small white dots from mineral residue when hard water dries on leaves
