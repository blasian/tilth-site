## The 5 guide panels
1. Diagnostic test → Wet soil means wait; dry soil means water deeply
2. Overwatering signs → Wilting in saturated soil — roots have rotted; the plant can't take up water it has
3. Underwatering signs → Wilting that recovers within an hour of watering
4. Fix: overwatering → Stop watering — until soil dries an inch deep
5. Fix: underwatering → Water deeply, not often — a long soak that reaches the full root zone beats daily
