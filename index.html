DIAGNOSE

Why isn't
this growing?

Tap the plant. Describe what you're seeing. Tilth assembles every soil reading, weather event, and check-in the garden has logged, then asks Claude to reason through what's most likely happening — and what to do this week.

Not a chat window. A structured diagnosis with evidence from your own data: the nitrogen reading from last Tuesday, the dry stretch before the last rain, the transplant three weeks ago.

JOURNAL

A journal
that remembers.

Check in on plants with a photo and a note. Tilth stamps the weather, temperature, and humidity automatically, so months later you can see exactly what was happening when the basil was at its best.

Every reading, amendment, and transplant is dated. Every check-in carries its own conditions. Nothing has to be remembered because nothing is forgotten.

PRIVATE BY DEFAULT

Your data
stays yours.

No account. No cloud lock-in. No ad networks. Your plants, soil readings, and photos live on your device in a local database you own. Uninstall the app and it's gone — no server copy to chase.

Diagnoses send only the minimum context a plant question needs — species, bed, soil, weather, your concern. No name, no email, no identifier that ties any of it back to you.
