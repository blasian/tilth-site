# PNW Tomato Growing Guide

## Headline
**PNW Tomato Growing Guide**

## Subhead
*When to plant, what to grow, how to make it through Seattle summer*

## Hero
A healthy tomato plant in fruit — ripening cluster on the vine, with soft natural light suggesting overcast PNW conditions (not bright California sun). Cherry or small slicer varieties read better at thumbnail than beefsteak. Optional: terra-cotta pot or raised cedar bed visible at the base.

## Body — Quick facts (4 lines, mono labels + serif body)

```
PLANT    after May 1, nights above 50°F
SUN      8+ hours direct
WATER    1–2 in/wk, deep, mulched
SOIL     pH 6.0–6.8, well-draining
```

## Callout (terracotta accent)

> Choose early varieties — Sungold, Stupice, Cherokee Purple, Early Girl. Beefsteaks won't finish in a Seattle summer.
